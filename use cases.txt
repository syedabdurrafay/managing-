class timetable is a structured schedule that outlines the timing and sequence of academic classes for a student. 
It typically includes details such as the days of the week, specific times for each class, and the corresponding subjects or courses.
The timetable serves as a visual aid to help students organize and manage their academic commitments,
ensuring they attend classes at the designated times and allowing for effective planning of study sessions and other activities.
